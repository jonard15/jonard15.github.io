Social Media Application

The application enables users to create and view posts through a responsive design that works seamlessly across mobile and desktop devices. It can be downloaded and installed for both platforms, offering a smooth user experience whether online or offline. Users have the option to receive notifications based on their preferences, ensuring personalized engagement without being disruptive.

Used Technologies:

  • VueJs
  • Quasar
  • PWA
  • NodeJs
  • ExpressJs
  • Firebase
